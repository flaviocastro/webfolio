Introduction

CLEVER SOLUTIONS is a global company with strong innovative spirit, it's structured around a management strategy that focuses heavily on the motivation of its employees.

In April 2015 , they launched a second version of ambiPortal , one of the most successful portals. The goal of ambiPortal is to provide companies with the simplest and fastest way to recycle their wastes.

They designed and sent it to me a new front page for the Portal. But the design made was for a non responsive website and from an UI/UX perspective, the page didn't push the right buttons for the users that they would have.

So I gave them my vision, a simple web page, with the right structure and the action calls in the right places.
